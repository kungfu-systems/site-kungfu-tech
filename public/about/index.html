About Kungfu

A commercial product line for real-world work with agents.

Kungfu is preparing a local-first control pane for long-running agent work. The public product story belongs on kungfu.tech; developer-facing material lives at libkungfu.dev.

What Kungfu is preparing

Kungfu is being prepared to help users see whether delegated agent work is progressing, blocked, ready, done, or wasting tokens and runtime.

The product direction connects cost, state, proof, and next actions instead of asking users to trust an opaque dashboard or a self-reported completion claim.

Commercial stewardship

Kungfu is commercially operated by Kungfu Origin Technology Limited.

The business model is intended to build on open-source trust, official artifacts, optional services, support, governance, and future team or cloud capabilities rather than private-code scarcity.

Developer axis

Developers and agents need the same first-party technical facts: runtime behavior, contracts, package surfaces, and trust documentation.

That material is intentionally separated at libkungfu.dev so this site can stay focused on the product and commercial axis.

Current availability

Kungfu is being prepared in the open. This site describes product direction and stewardship, not a claim that all shown product capabilities are already generally available.

Follow the open-source core and public issues on GitHub.
