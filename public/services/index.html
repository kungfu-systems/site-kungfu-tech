Services

Commercial services will build around an open, local-first core.

Kungfu is not generally released yet. This page describes the intended service areas so visitors can understand the commercial direction without mistaking previews for shipped product features.

Product stewardship

Official product direction, release gates, artifacts, documentation, and issue triage are part of the commercial stewardship surface.

Public collaboration currently starts from the open-source repository.

Trust and governance

Kungfu's service direction is tied to local facts, auditability, provider compliance, and honest cost attribution.

Future governance capabilities should help teams understand responsibility state without making the local core depend on a hosted login.

Team and cloud capabilities

Future commercial services may provide optional team coordination, compute, storage, support, or hosted-compatible adapters.

Those services should remain additive to the local-first workflow rather than replacing it with another opaque control plane.
