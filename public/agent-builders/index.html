01 · How libkungfu sees action

An action is a loop through reality, not a row in a workflow.

libkungfu preserves the coordinates needed to act without losing intent, evidence, authority, or continuity.

  1. Fact Cut NWhat is admitted now?

    A rooted view of accepted state, not every observed claim.

  2. Action GeometryWhy, from what view, under whose authority?
    • Pursuit · why
    • Atlas · what is known
    • Warrant · what is allowed
  3. ActionBindingBind the exact decision inputs.

    Changing a Fact, intent, view, permission, action, or resource creates a different binding.

  4. ActCross into the real world.

    A tool or RPC success signal does not settle meaning.

  5. EpisodeWhat actually happened?

    Causal occurrence, artifacts, receipts, consequences, recovery, and proof.

  6. Inspect + admitWhich consequences become Facts?

    Local policy admits, rejects, degrades, or conflicts new claims.

  7. Fact Cut N+1What may safely happen next?

    The successor cut starts the next loop without rewriting history.

02 · Why KFD can support plural Hubs

KFD connects Hub boundaries. It does not become the Hub.

Every participant keeps its product, identity, policy, storage, models, and customer relationship. KFD standardizes only the responsibility exchange between them.

Participant-owned control plane

Hub A · libkungfu adopter

  1. Vendor UI, models, accounts, workflows
  2. Local identity, policy, and admission
  3. libkungfu Facts, Episodes, and recovery
  4. KFD adapter, exchange store, verdict writer
KFD responsibility boundary
  1. Negotiate exact capabilities
  2. Propose rooted responsibility
  3. Deliver through any binding
  4. Assess and verdict locally

Local call · IPC · file · HTTP · gRPC · message bus · offline bundle

Participant-owned control plane

Hub B · independent implementation

  1. Participant UX and workflows
  2. Local identity, policy, and admission
  3. Any conforming runtime and evidence store
  4. KFD adapter, exchange store, verdict writer

No central KFD authority: no required global identity provider, Hub registry, database, transport, clock, or KFD cloud.

Delivery ≠ Admission

Bytes arrived; the receiver still owns the verdict.

Occurrence ≠ Completion

An Episode happened; success still needs independent assessment.

Authentication ≠ Authority

Identity control does not prove permission, truth, or fact admission.

Kungfu Cloud is a future option, not a current requirement.

KFD is designed for plural Hubs. A future Kungfu Cloud may implement the same protocol, but adopting libkungfu or KFD does not require it and this page does not announce a cloud product.
