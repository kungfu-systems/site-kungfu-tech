Adoption architecture

Your product and Hub stay yours.

The layers have explicit ownership. libkungfu and KFD add runtime facts and portable evidence; they do not take over the vendor’s product surface or customer relationship.

01

Your Agent product

Vendor-owned

TUI or GUI, models, accounts, permissions, provider routing, and product experience.

02

libkungfu local or worker runtime

Open runtime layer

Runtime facts, Episode lifecycle, recovery, and bounded local evidence without default prompt or credential upload.

03

KFD open protocol

Topology-neutral

Portable reports, conformance semantics, and exchange between local, remote, or hybrid implementations.

04

Your Hub

Vendor-owned

Cloud workflows, fleet coordination, commercial services, and the customer relationship.

Kungfu Cloud is a future option, not a current requirement.

KFD is designed for plural Hubs. A future Kungfu Cloud may implement the same protocol, but adopting libkungfu or KFD does not require it and this page does not announce a cloud product.
